APP UI Mockup

This mockup below is a basic design of my app. It shows a map window on the right, with options on the left where users will be able to make their choices to select the display and data they are interested in.

The options on the left will include interest of time and location. Location can include aquifer, county, or drawing an area on the map to select the wells whithin. The displayed data will be an interpolation from both time and location. The surfaces are calculated from underground water level from wells.

For example, the outlined box is the selected area where the yellow dots illustrates the wells on the map. And in the picture below, it demonstrates the underground water level represented by color on the date of 01/19/2010 as selected on the left.

I am also thinking of doing a button called “adv” where you can set desired colors that will be shown on the map.
